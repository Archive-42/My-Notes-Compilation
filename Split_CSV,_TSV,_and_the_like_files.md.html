Tab-separated values when not surrounded by single or double quotes 

[^\t"']+|"([^"]*)"|'([^']*)'

[^\s"']+|"([^"]*)"|'([^']*)'

Drop-in csv/tsv pattern

/\t(?=(?:[^\"]*\"[^\"]*\")*[^\"]*$)/gm;

Just replace the first character, that acts as a separator (here, a tab).
